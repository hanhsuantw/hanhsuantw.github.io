SKI RESORT

POWDER SNOW HEAVEN FOR SKIERS AND SNOWBOARDERS

Furano is one of Japan's most famous ski resorts, although relatively little is known about it outside of Japan. Situated in the middle of Hokkaido, Japan's northernmost island, Furano benefits from Siberian storm systems that pass over the Sea of Japan and then dump the lightest and driest snow in all of Hokkaido - and 9 meters of it each season. After a big snow fall there is often untracked powder for days.

JOIN THE FESTIVALS AND FEEL THE NATURE WITH FUN

Joining festivals will be great exeriences with all the Furao local people and also of course interesting and great memories of your vacation in Furano. Foremore, outdoor activities are available to join in Furano resort! We have many tours to suit whatever you may be looking for. Get together with your friends and family to have some fantastic nature adventures here.

FURANO FIREWORKS

The best place to see the fireworks is by the river, but make sure to claim a spot early as this show attracts close to one million people. Expect crowds, traffic and lines at food stalls and bathrooms. As the pyrotechnics begin around 7:00 p.m., it is best to arrive a couple hours early to find a place to sit.

BELLY BUTTON

Hokkai Heso Matsuri aka the Belly Button Festival is a fun-filled event that originated in the city of Furano around 1969. It started off as a simple dance festival, but later became a humorous and creative event with an aim to bring unity through laughter.

HOT AIR BALLOON

We rise to 1000m and float over Furano and close to the Taisetusan Mountain Range. This is one thing you should do at least once in your lifetime. At this altitude you'll often see diamond dust, which makes it appear as though thousands of diamond are flying around you.

BIKE RIDING

Rent a bike or bring your own, and get ready to explore the city’s parks and attractions with one of these leisurely routes. If you’re feeling adventurous, you can even plot your own path with the map of Furano’s bike trails.
